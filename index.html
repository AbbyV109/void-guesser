How to play

Identify the brane before you run out of locusts.

Every guess will give you feedback and expand the viewable area.

The possible guesses will update accordingly.

Brane resets every day at 5 a.m. UTC.

Good Luck!

Tips and tricks

Remember that the first brane of a domain is the mural room and the second is the statue room.

Every tree brane counts as any other tree brane.

Every Lillie brane that isn't unique has been eliminated.

If your guess includes the letter "L" it will only display Lillie rooms in the suggestions.

Suggestions and guesses aren't case sensitive.

All assets used are from Void Stranger.
